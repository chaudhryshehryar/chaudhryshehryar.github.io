An electronics hobbyist, average programmer, and a computer science student at the
University of Technology Sydney.


"Computer Science is no more about computers than astronomy is about telescopes," Edsger Dijkstra.
This theory has been revolving in my mind for a very long time. Something about that rectangular box of information
has always intrigued me. While others were busy playing Need for Speed special editions and fighting whose turn it was
to take racing cars on tracks or completing missions in GTA, my mind occupied by theories of how the computer might be
doing all this. Of how computers worked, why they worked, and what else they could do?
Perhaps I was interested in the motion of the stars. Not with the telescope.
